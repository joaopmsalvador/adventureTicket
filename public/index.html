About

The mission of the Adventure Ticket is to match unused tickets to adventure seakers with the same first nameand last name.

Tickets are intransmisable, unless you find one with the same first and last name as yours. You don't believe me? Check this story.

Based on that story I got a simple Idea. To find a way to occupy those empty plane, cruise, train seats that someone purchased and will not use because he or her plans have changed. For me those empty seats represent a wasted travel opportunitie. And because when I was young my parents told me we should never waste stuff I decided to build this website. To figth unused seat waste and to promote adventure to the world :)

How can this idea possibly work?

- First we need sad people. People who are sad because their plans changed and the ticket purchaser doesn't give them any refund. If you are one of this persons theAdventureTicket.com is really sorry for you.

- Sad people can upload their ticket and promote a travel opportunitie to the world just because they can. (or because of this reasons)

- Everyone can subscribe as an adventure seaker, even sad people who will not use their ticket.

- Finally once there is a match from a sad person's name who uploaded their ticket and an adventure seaker an automatic email will put both in contact. You will decide the terms. The ticket can be given, sold or exchanged for a coffee, but it's your job to make this opportunitie a reallity.

- Remember once you subscribe theAdventureTicket.com at any time on your life you can receive an email with a random travel experience. If suddenly you realise you don't like to travel anymore you can unsubscribe.
